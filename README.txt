With this update player is able to:
    - Walk
        - Side to side
        - Forward and backwards
        - Character is animating and stops animating if no keys are pressed (character then reverts back to the idle position)
    - Switch camera modes
        - DEFAULT - (can look around with arrow keys but the movement is turned off)
        - FIRST_PERSON - (can look around and the camera position is attached to the head position)
        - THIRD_PERSON - (can move freely with arrow keys and 'f' and 'v' buttons as descibed in the rubric)
